Introduction back to ToC

The EM-KPI ontology is developed to exchange the key performance information and master data in district and buildings. The ontology contains two parts, the KPI (key performance indicator) part and the EM master data part, which represents the multi-level performance information for energy performance tracking and integrates the master data domains for data exploitation, respectively. The demonstrator of the ontology indicates that the ontology integrates well the master data, and link it effectively to the KPIs for performance analysis. Moreover, the generated Linked Data proves its feasibility to interchange data among different stakeholders and explore the insights for performance improvement.
